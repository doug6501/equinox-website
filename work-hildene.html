Hildene Lincoln Hall Wedding

Creating a magical atmosphere for a summer wedding at a historic Vermont venue.

The Vision

The goal was to enhance the natural beauty of Hildene's Lincoln Hall without overpowering it. The client wanted a romantic, elegant, and celebratory atmosphere, with crystal-clear audio for toasts and a dynamic stage setup for the live band that would keep guests dancing all night.

The Solution

Our team designed a comprehensive plan that included discreet audio reinforcement using high-quality speakers that blended into the venue's architecture. For the reception, we created a custom atmospheric lighting design with warm, elegant uplighting and intelligent dance floor lighting. We provided a full backline and stage setup for the band, ensuring a professional and energetic performance.
