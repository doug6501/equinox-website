Hildene Lincoln Hall Wedding

Transforming a historic venue into a magical summer reception.

The Vision

For a beautiful summer wedding at the historic Hildene estate, the vision was to create a warm, magical, and energetic atmosphere for the reception inside Lincoln Hall. The goal was to complement the venue's natural elegance while providing a full-production experience for a live band and over 200 guests.

Our Solution

We designed and executed a comprehensive production plan that balanced sophistication with celebration. Our team provided a full stage setup for the live band, complete with a professional audio system that delivered crystal-clear sound without overpowering the space. The key to the transformation was our lighting design, which used a combination of warm uplighting to highlight the hall's architecture and dynamic, color-changeable fixtures to create an exciting dance floor atmosphere. We ensured every element was seamlessly integrated, preserving the venue's historic charm while delivering a modern, unforgettable party.

Project Details

  • Client: Private Wedding
  • Venue: Hildene, The Lincoln Family Home
  • Location: Manchester, VT
  • Date: Summer 2025

Services Provided

  • Live Band Audio Reinforcement
  • Atmospheric & Dance Floor Lighting
  • Power Distribution
  • On-site Technical Support
