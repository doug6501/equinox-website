Kimpton Taconic Wedding

Crafting a warm and elegant atmosphere for a winter wedding reception.

The Vision

The client envisioned a sophisticated and cozy winter wedding reception. They wanted to transform the beautiful ballroom at the Kimpton Taconic into an intimate, celebratory space that felt both elegant and welcoming for their guests.

The Solution

Our team focused on creating a layered lighting design that brought warmth to the space. We used soft, amber uplighting to highlight the room's architectural features and pin-spotting to illuminate the floral centerpieces on each table. A full sound system was provided for the DJ, ensuring excellent audio quality that was clear for speeches and powerful for the dance floor.
