Kimpton Taconic Wedding

Crafting a perfect audio-visual experience in a boutique hotel setting.

The Vision

A winter wedding at the beautiful Kimpton Taconic hotel required an AV solution that was both elegant and effective. The client's priority was ensuring that every moment, from the vows during the ceremony to the heartfelt toasts at the reception, was perfectly seen and heard by every guest in an intimate and sophisticated setting.

Our Solution

We provided a complete AV package tailored to the unique spaces within the hotel. This included a discreet sound system with wireless microphones for the ceremony, ensuring pristine audio quality. For the reception, we installed elegant uplighting to enhance the room's warm ambiance and a high-quality sound system for dinner music and dancing. Our team handled all technical aspects with minimal intrusion, allowing the couple and their guests to fully immerse themselves in the celebration.
