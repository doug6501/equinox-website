Bennington Museum Gala

Creating an elegant and immersive atmosphere for a prestigious annual dinner.

The Vision

For their prestigious annual gala dinner, the Bennington Museum required a sophisticated and seamless audio-visual solution. The goal was to enhance the beautiful event space, ensure clear communication during presentations, and create an elegant, immersive atmosphere for their most important patrons and guests.

Our Solution

We deployed multiple large, high-definition video displays throughout the venue, strategically placed for optimal viewing without disrupting the event's aesthetic. Each screen showcased a rotating gallery of artwork and sponsor recognition loops. We complemented the visuals with a distributed audio system for perfectly intelligible speeches and ambient background music. Our team worked closely with the museum staff to ensure every technical element was discreetly integrated, preserving the focus on the art and the evening's program.

Project Details

  • Client: Bennington Museum
  • Venue: Bennington Museum
  • Location: Bennington, VT
  • Date: December 2024

Services Provided

  • HD Video Displays
  • Distributed Audio System
  • Presentation Support
  • Ambient Lighting
  • On-site Technical Management
