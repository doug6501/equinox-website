Bennington Museum Gala

Designing a sophisticated and immersive environment for a premier fundraising event.

The Vision

For their most important fundraising event of the year, the Bennington Museum needed to transform their space into an elegant and engaging environment. The technical production had to be flawless, supporting keynote speakers, a silent auction, and live entertainment while reflecting the prestige of the institution.

The Solution

We implemented a multi-faceted solution that included strategic placement of high-definition video displays for sponsor recognition and auction updates. Our team designed a custom lighting scheme to complement the museum's architecture and create a warm, inviting atmosphere. A distributed audio system ensured that every guest could hear the evening's presentations perfectly, from the front row to the back of the room.
