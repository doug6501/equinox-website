The Challenge

For the premiere of the historical documentary "Vanish," which explores the disappearing barns of historic Vermont, the Bennington Museum needed to transform their education center into a true cinema. The goal was to provide an immersive viewing experience that honored the film's beautiful cinematography and important local story.

The Solution

Equinox Audio Visuals designed and executed a complete cinematic presentation solution. We installed a large-format, professional-grade screen and a high-lumen projector to ensure a bright, crisp image. A multi-channel surround sound system was deployed to create an immersive audio environment, capturing every detail of the film's score and narration. Our team handled all setup, testing, and operation, allowing the museum and filmmakers to focus on their audience and a successful premiere.
