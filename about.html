Our Story is in the Work.

Equinox was born in the mountains of Vermont with a simple idea: event production could be better. Better tech, yes, but also a better experience for our clients. In an industry of black boxes and backstage crews, we bring a hospitality mindset to a tech-heavy world. We believe how you get there matters just as much as the final result.

We're based in Manchester, but our work and our reputation take us around the world. No matter the location, we bring the same level of dedication, craftsmanship, and our signature "cool kids, nice guys" approach to every project we're proud to put our name on.

The Equinox Process

A great event doesn't happen by accident. It's the result of a disciplined, collaborative process. Here’s how we ensure success from the first call to the final curtain.

01

Discovery & Design

It all starts with listening. We dive deep into your goals, your audience, and your vision. Then, our team designs a comprehensive technical solution that's creative, reliable, and perfectly aligned with your budget.

02

Pre-Production & Prep

This is where the magic happens. We meticulously prep and test every piece of equipment, coordinate with venues and vendors, and build a detailed run-of-show that leaves nothing to chance.

03

Flawless Execution

On-site, our team is the calm, steady hand you can rely on. We manage every technical cue with precision and professionalism, proactively solving challenges and ensuring a seamless experience for you and your attendees.

04

Post-Event Debrief

Our partnership doesn't end when the lights come up. We provide a thorough post-event debrief, discussing what worked, what we can improve, and how we can make your next event even better.
